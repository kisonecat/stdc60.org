## Info for Invited Plenary and Semi-Plenary Speakers

The conference organizers expect to cover travel expenses for invited Plenary and
Semi-Plenary Speakers. Please use American-based airlines for your travel.

If you have any other sources of support, please let us know so that we may use
these funds to support travel for junior participants instead.
