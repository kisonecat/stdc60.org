title: Geometric Group Theory
---

**Organizers:** TBA

Geometric group theory studies groups through their actions on metric and topological spaces.

Sample topics:

- Hyperbolic groups and their boundaries: a finitely generated group $G$ is hyperbolic
  iff its Cayley graph satisfies the $\delta$-slim triangles condition for some $\delta \geq 0$
- Acylindrical hyperbolicity and its consequences for group structure
- Mapping class groups of surfaces and curve complexes
- CAT(0) cube complexes and their applications
