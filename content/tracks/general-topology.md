---
title: General and Set-Theoretic Topology
---

**Organizers:** TBA

This track covers set-theoretic and combinatorial techniques in general topology.

Sample topics:

- Consistency results: e.g., under $\mathsf{MA} + \neg\mathsf{CH}$,
  all separable metrizable spaces of cardinality $< \mathfrak{c}$ are Lindelöf.
- Cardinal invariants of the continuum: $\mathfrak{b}$, $\mathfrak{d}$,
  $\mathfrak{p}$, $\mathfrak{t}$, and their combinatorial characterizations
- Selection principles and topological games
- Covering properties: Menger, Rothberger, Hurewicz
