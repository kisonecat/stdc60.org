title: Continuum Theory
---

**Organizers:** TBA

Continuum theory studies compact connected metric spaces (continua) and their mappings.

Sample topics:

- Characterizations of the pseudo-arc and other hereditarily indecomposable continua
- Inverse limits: $\varprojlim (X_i, f_i)$ and their topological properties
- Fixed-point theorems for continua: if $f : X \to X$ is a map of a tree-like continuum,
  does $f$ have a fixed point?
- Span, surjective span, and related concepts for continua
