title: Dynamical Systems
---

**Organizers:** TBA

This track covers topological and combinatorial aspects of dynamical systems,
including iteration of maps on continua, complex dynamics, and symbolic dynamics.

Sample topics:

- Thurston's characterization of postcritically finite rational maps
- Dynamics on dendrites and trees: for $f: T \to T$, the entropy satisfies
  $h(f) = \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{1}{n} \log |L_n(f)|$
- Lamination models for polynomial Julia sets
- Rotation theory for maps of the circle and annulus
