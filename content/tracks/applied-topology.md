title: Applied Topology and Topological Data Analysis
---

**Organizers:** TBA

This track covers applications of topology to data analysis and other applied domains,
including persistent homology, Mapper, topological descriptors, and connections to
machine learning.

Sample topics:

- Persistent homology and its stability: if $f, g : X \to \mathbb{R}$ satisfy
  $\|f - g\|_\infty \leq \varepsilon$, then $d_b(D_f, D_g) \leq \varepsilon$.
- Vietoris–Rips complexes and their homotopy types
- Topological methods in time-series and shape analysis
