title: Geometric Topology
---

**Organizers:** TBA

Geometric topology studies manifolds, knots, and related structures using both
algebraic and geometric methods.

Sample topics:

- Thurston geometrization and the Poincaré conjecture: every closed simply connected
  3-manifold is homeomorphic to $S^3$
- Dehn surgery and knot invariants: Alexander polynomial $\Delta_K(t)$,
  Jones polynomial $V_K(t)$
- $4$-manifolds: Freedman's theorem and exotic $\mathbb{R}^4$
- Floer homology and its topological applications
